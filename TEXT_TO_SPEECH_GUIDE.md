# Text-to-Speech Quick Start Guide

## 🎧 What's New?

The CyberSaarthi app now has a **"Read Aloud"** feature! You can click a speaker icon to have any text read aloud in your chosen language.

## 🎯 Where to Use It

### Learning Modules
- Click on any lesson question to expand it
- Look for the speaker icon 🔊 next to the question
- Click to hear the question read aloud
- Click again next to the answer to hear the explanation

### Quiz
- During quiz questions, click the speaker icon next to the question
- Useful for questions about identifying scams or deepfakes

## �n Operating Instructions

### Basic Controls
- **Speaker Icon (🔊)** - Click to play text in your language
- **Pause Icon (⏸)** - Appears while playing, click to pause
- **X Icon (❌)** - Click to stop and reset

### Step-by-Step

1. **Play Audio**
   - Click the speaker 🔊 button
   - The button turns blue
   - Text starts reading aloud

2. **Pause Audio**
   - Click the blue pause ⏸ button
   - Audio stops momentarily

3. **Continue Playing**
   - Click the play ▶️ button
   - Audio resumes from where it paused

4. **Stop Completely**
   - Click the red X ❌ button
   - Audio stops and resets

## 🌍 Supported Languages

The app detects your language preference automatically:
- **English** - Reads in English (US)
- **हिन्दी** - Reads in Hindi (India)
- **मराठी** - Reads in Marathi (India)
- **தமிழ்** - Reads in Tamil (India)

Your language preference is automatically used for read-aloud!

## ⚙️ Settings

The text is read at:
- **Speed:** Normal (slightly slower for clarity)
- **Volume:** Device volume
- **Voice:** System default voice for your language

All these settings use your device's native voice, so no internet is required!

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Button not appearing?
- Your browser might not support text-to-speech
- Try Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- The feature won't work in Internet Explorer

### Text not reading?
- Check your device volume
- Make sure the browser tab has focus
- Try reloading the page
- Ensure your browser has audio permission

### Accent sounds wrong?
- Different languages have different voice quality depending on your device
- This uses your system's built-in voices
- Quality varies by device and OS

### Can't hear anything?
1. Check device volume isn't muted
2. Check browser volume in system mixer
3. Try a different app's speaker first to test device audio
4. Reload the page and try again
